Invoice UBL & Bank Statement Export

Bulk export invoices to Peppol BIS 3.0 UBL XML and bank statements to PDF

Key Features

UBL XML Export

Export customer invoices, vendor bills, and credit notes to Peppol BIS 3.0 compliant UBL XML files.

Bank Statements PDF

Export bank statements from multiple accounts to PDF format in a single ZIP archive.

Quarter Selection

Export all documents from a specific quarter (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4) with one click.

Quarterly Auto-Send

Automatically send exports via email at the start of each quarter to your accountant or BilltoBox.

Quarterly Auto-Send

Configure automatic quarterly exports in Settings > Accounting > Quarterly Export

  • UBL files to BilltoBox: Send all invoices and bills as UBL XML to your BilltoBox import email
  • Bank statements to Accountant: Send PDF statements to your accountant
  • Configurable send day: Choose which day of the month to send (1-28)
  • Multiple bank accounts: Select which accounts to include

Peppol BIS 3.0 Compliant

This module generates Peppol BIS Billing 3.0 compliant UBL XML files, following the EN 16931 European standard.

Compatible with all Belgian accounting software:

BilltoBox • ClearFacts • Exact • Yuki • Octopus • and more...

Belgian E-invoicing 2026

Important: From January 1, 2026, e-invoicing becomes mandatory for all B2B transactions in Belgium. This module helps you prepare by generating Peppol-compliant UBL files that your accountant can import.

How It Works

1

2

3
